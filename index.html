About RVIT Campus:-

In the Memory of Late Shri Ranveer Singh, R V Institute of Technology, Bijnor (RVIT) was established in the year 2009 for the purpose of imparting technical education and human values in the young Technocrats.It is one of the Best Engineering College in Bijnor, UP.
Progressively, RV Institute of Pharmacy (RVIP) was started with a noble idea to equip students with the best knowledge and skills of pharmaceuticals, nurture scientific temper, identify hidden talents, inculcate professional ethics and human values and enable students to realize their full potential.Thus our motto is to mold students into a professionally superior and morally strong global manpower with the ability to adapt to the changing environment intellectually and technologically and become the leaders of the future.
The Institute has lush green campus and occupying more than 16 acres land on the State highway (76). The noise and pollution free environment acts as a stimulant for academic activities. The campus has planned buildings, laboratories, library, hostels and sports complex that catches the eye of every passerby.
“Our Philosophy is to invest all the resources towards student’s cause and development”
